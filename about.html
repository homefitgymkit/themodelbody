My story

Built from real life, not a fad.

After years of overtraining, chasing trends and feeling exhausted by diets that never lasted, I built something different. The Model Body Method is smart strength training that creates a lean, feminine shape without spiking cortisol or burning you out.

It was never about restriction. It's about training with intention, fuelling your body properly, and building routines that lift your energy, posture and confidence for good. The kind of plan you can actually keep.

Now I share it all: monthly plans inside the Club, one-off guides, 1:1 coaching and the free gifts that started it. Wherever you are, there's a way to train with me.
