Hey, I’m Kassy 🍒

Model, influencer, and fully qualified personal trainer. I’ve worked with Victoria’s Secret, Prada, and Chanel.

After years of overtraining and trend-chasing, I created The Model Body Method: smart strength training that builds a toned, feminine physique without raising cortisol or burning out.

This isn’t about restriction. It’s about training with intention, fuelling your body properly, and building routines that boost your energy, posture, and confidence.

What You’ll Be Eating 🍒

Simple, balanced meals designed to keep you lean and full of energy

🥪

Pre-Gym Smoothie

Plant milk, frozen banana & protein powder. Quick fuel before you train.

🍳

High-Protein Breakfast

Boiled eggs, seeded bread, chia & flax seeds. 30g protein to start strong.

🍣

Sushi Bowl Lunch

Half rice, half cauliflower rice with salmon or shrimp & fresh veg.

🥗

Balanced Dinner

Your choice of protein with sweet potato, broccoli & greens.
